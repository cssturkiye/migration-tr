### 2. DATASET DESCRIPTION

**Dataset Name:** Migration-TR: Turkish Migration Discourse Dataset (2011-2022)  

**Corpus Size:** 5 884 624 Turkish-language tweets collected under the former Twitter Academic API.  

**Delivery Format:** *Hydrated* Post Objects **supplied in batches not exceeding 500 objects per Recipient per calendar day**, together with derived annotations.  

**Derived Elements:** timestamps, anonymised user metadata (excluding clear @handles per GDPR data-minimisation), engagement metrics, topic labels, sentiment scores, bot-probability scores. **Derived annotations contain no direct identifiers.**

### 4. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

**4.1 X.com Developer Policy**  
* Provider will deliver hydrated Tweets **only by non-automated means** (e-mail, SFTP, physical media).  
* **Per-Recipient daily cap:** 500 hydrated Post/User Objects.  
* Recipient must delete or obscure any Tweet that X or the author deletes, protects, or otherwise suppresses **within 24 hours of notice**; Provider will send such notices by e-mail, and Recipient must comply within the same 24 hours.  
* Provider monitors X's Compliance API to identify deletion or protection events in near real time.  
* No public redistribution of hydrated text.

**4.2 Data-protection & Ethics**  
* Processing must comply with GDPR Art. 5 principles, KVKK Art. 4, and relevant institutional ethics approvals.  
* Recipient shall implement data-minimisation and pseudonymisation; user handles must not be published in clear text.
